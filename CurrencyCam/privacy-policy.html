CurrencyCam is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access

CurrencyCam requires camera access to detect currency amounts in real-time. Camera images are processed entirely on your device and are never stored, uploaded, or transmitted to any server.

User Preferences

Your currency selections and custom exchange rates are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, images, or camera recordings

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

CurrencyCam uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), subject to your App Tracking Transparency choice
  • IP address (for approximate location-based ad serving)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

3. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, CurrencyCam will ask for your permission before tracking you across other apps and websites. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your activity across other apps.

4. Data Security

All currency detection and conversion processing occurs entirely on your device. No image data or conversion results are transmitted over the internet. Your custom exchange rate settings are stored locally and protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms.

5. Children's Privacy

CurrencyCam does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: lj_dev@ymail.ne.jp

ユーザー設定

通貨の選択やカスタム為替レートは、iOSの標準ストレージ（UserDefaults）を使用してデバイスにローカル保存されます。これらのデータがデバイスの外に出ることはありません。

以下の情報は一切収集しません：

  • 氏名、メールアドレス、連絡先情報
  • 位置情報
  • トラッキング目的のデバイス識別子

  • 写真、画像、カメラの録画データ

Google AdMob

為替カメラは広告表示のためにGoogle AdMobを使用しています。AdMobはGoogleのプライバシーポリシーに記載されたデータを収集する場合があります：

  • デバイス情報（機種、OSバージョン）
  • 広告識別子（IDFA）※Appトラッキング透明性の選択に従います

パーソナライズド広告は、iOSの設定 > プライバシーとセキュリティ > トラッキングからオプトアウトできます。

4. データセキュリティ

通貨の検出と換算処理はすべてデバイス上で完結します。画像データや換算結果がインターネットを通じて送信されることはありません。カスタム為替レートの設定はローカルに保存され、iOS内蔵のセキュリティ機構により保護されています。
